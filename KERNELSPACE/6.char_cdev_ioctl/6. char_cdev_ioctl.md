# Program 6 - cdev Based Driver
Purpose: cdev_init(), cdev_add(), modern character driver registration.

Interview Questions
Q1: Why not use read/write?

Answer:

read/write transfer stream data.

ioctl is used for control/configuration operations.
Q2: Why copy_from_user inside ioctl?

Answer:

arg is a userspace pointer.

Kernel cannot directly dereference userspace memory.
Q3: Why unlocked_ioctl and not ioctl?

Answer:

Older kernels used:

.ioctl

Modern kernels use:

.unlocked_ioctl

because Big Kernel Lock (BKL) was removed.

Q4: What is arg?
long my_ioctl(..., unsigned long arg)

arg can be:

integer value
pointer
structure pointer

Most drivers treat it as a userspace pointer.
