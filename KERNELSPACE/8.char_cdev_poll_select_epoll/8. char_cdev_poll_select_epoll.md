## Solution

poll()

Driver:
poll_wait()

Writer:
wake_up_interruptible()

Interview Answer
"I used wake_up_interruptible() and only one process received data. Why?"

Answer:
wake_up_interruptible() wakes all eligible waiters.
However only one process may consume the shared data.
The others can wake up, find no data available, and go back to sleep.
Therefore userspace may appear to show only one process receiving the event even though multiple processes were awakened internally.
